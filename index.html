Theory Group Meeting

About the Meeting

Theory group meetings are a place for blackboard talks (slides are welcome, of course). We encourage exploration of all aspects of theoretical astrophysics. People are supposed to elaborate on theories and clearly explain their formulae. Feel free to dive deeply into details of fundamental astrophysical theories. Presentations don’t necessarily have to be your most recent research updates. Paper review or any interesting piece of physics in your mind are also welcome. Let’s have fun!
