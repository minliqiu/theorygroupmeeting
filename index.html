Theory Group Meeting

About

Theory group meetings are a place for blackboard talks (slides are welcome, of course). We encourage exploration of all aspects of theoretical astrophysics. Feel free to dive deeply into details of fundamental astrophysical theories. Presentations don’t necessarily have to be your most recent research updates. Paper review or any interesting piece of physics in your mind are also welcome. One meeting usually consists of one or two 20-25min talk + 5-10min discussion + (optional) 5-10min paper review/research update/miscellaneous topics. Let’s have fun!
